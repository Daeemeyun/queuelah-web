Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

QueueLah ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that provides crowd-sourced queue reporting for hawker centres, food courts, and restaurants in Singapore. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used for authentication and account recovery.
  • Username — a display name you choose, shown on your public profile, leaderboard, and forum posts.
  • Queue reports — queue level and wait time you submit for eateries. Reports are associated with your account while your account is active.
  • Forum posts and reviews — content you post publicly in the app.
  • Avatar customisation — accessories and frame choices stored against your profile.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location (precise) — used on-device to show nearby eateries on the map and calculate distances. Location is not stored on our servers.
  • Device push token — collected when you grant notification permission, used to send streak reminders and report confirmation notifications.
  • Usage data — screen views, feature interactions, and in-app events collected via PostHog for product analytics. This data is pseudonymous and used to improve the app.
  • Crash reports — error logs and stack traces collected via Sentry when the app crashes. May include your user ID so we can investigate account-specific issues.
  • Advertising identifiers — your device's advertising ID is used by Google AdMob to serve banner and rewarded advertisements within the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the QueueLah service
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display live queue information contributed by the community
  • To calculate your points, streak, badges, and leaderboard ranking
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (streak reminders, report confirmations)
  • To analyse how the app is used and improve its features
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and real-time infrastructure. Data is stored on servers in the Asia Pacific region. Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — map display and eatery location data. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising. AdMob may use your advertising identifier to serve personalised ads. Privacy Policy
  • PostHog — product analytics. Collects pseudonymous usage events. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — crash reporting and error monitoring. Privacy Policy
  • Expo — push notification delivery infrastructure. Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Queue reports are retained in anonymised form after account deletion to preserve the integrity of community data. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 6).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 3 (service providers) or as required by law.

Queue reports, forum posts, reviews, and your username are visible to other users of the app as part of the community features.

6. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings or the in-app Settings screen

To delete your account, go to Settings → Delete Account within the app. This will permanently remove your account, profile, badges, forum posts, and reviews. Queue reports will be anonymised rather than deleted so community data is preserved.

7. Children's Privacy

QueueLah is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via a notice in the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
